Progressive unfreezing improved performance compared to both the baseline CNN and feature extraction.

The model achieved 73.05% accuracy and a macro F1 score of 0.62, showing a clear improvement over Week 2.

The model performed well across most classes, including minority classes, and achieved strong performance on the majority class (nv).

Confusion between bkl and mel was reduced compared to previous weeks, improving reliability of predictions.

These results show that allowing deeper layers of the pretrained model to adapt leads to better performance than keeping the backbone frozen.
