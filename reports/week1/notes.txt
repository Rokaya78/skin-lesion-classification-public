Baseline CNN achieved ~51% accuracy and macro F1 of 0.26.
The model performed well on the majority class (nv) but struggled on minority classes such as bkl and vasc.

Significant confusion exists between bkl and mel, with many benign lesions misclassified as melanoma.
This reflects the difficulty of distinguishing visually similar skin conditions.

The results highlight the limitations of training from scratch on an imbalanced dataset and motivate the use of transfer learning in later stages.
